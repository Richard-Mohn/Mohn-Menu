## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity
Restaurants are bleeding 25-30% in fees to DoorDash, Uber Eats, and GrubHub. Home-based businesses have no modern solution. Small local shops can't compete with big tech platforms. **LOCL is the answer.**

### The Solution
LOCL is a **white-label, customizable ordering & delivery platform** that restaurants, convenience stores, home-based businesses, and makers can deploy instantly with:
- **Zero per-order fees** (only setup + optional monthly support)
- **Full customer data ownership** (no platform middleman)
- **Real-time delivery management** (use your own drivers)
- **Built-in loyalty & marketing tools**
- **Live order tracking & fulfillment visibility**

### The Vision
Position LOCL as the **"Operating System for Local Businesses"** ‚Äî the anti-corporate, high-margin alternative to delivery platforms that takes 30% of every order.

### The Business Model
1. **High One-Time Setup Fee** ($297 - $1,499)
2. **Low/Optional Monthly Hosting** ($0 - $299/month)
3. **Support Tier Upsell** (Email, Phone, Training)
4. **No per-order fees ever** (we succeed when restaurants succeed)

**Why This Works:** Restaurants make more money ‚Üí they invest in more features ‚Üí they refer other restaurants.

### FEATURE FLAG ARCHITECTURE

In Firestore, a single `featureFlags` collection determines which UI components render:

```javascript
// In Firestore: /businesses/{businessId}/config/featureFlags
{
  businessType: 'restaurant', // determines what features are available
  features: {
    // Core Features (all tiers)
    ordering: true,
    paymentProcessing: true,
    customerNotifications: true,
    
    // Partner Tier+
    liveOrderTracking: true,
    driverManagement: true,
    analyticsBasic: true,
    loyaltyProgram: true,
    
    // Agency Tier+
    liveStreaming: true,
    batchRouting: true,
    advancedAnalytics: true,
    apiAccess: true,
    customIntegrations: true,
    
    // Business Type Specific
    kitchenDisplaySystem: businessType === 'restaurant',
    fulfillmentWorkflow: businessType === 'home_bakery' || businessType === 'crafter',
    subscriptionManagement: businessType === 'meal_prep' || businessType === 'farm_csa',
    customizationFields: businessType === 'crafter',
    batchDeliveryRouting: businessType === 'home_bakery' || businessType === 'meal_prep',
    cottageLawLabels: businessType === 'home_bakery' || businessType === 'farm_csa',
  }
}
```

This allows us to **run a single codebase** but show different features based on tier and business type.

#### Feature 2: Live Prep/Packing Livestream

**How It Works:**
1. Baker gets an order at 9 AM for 20 cookies
2. Baker taps "Go Live" in LOCL app
3. System generates a unique 30-minute livestream link
4. Customer gets SMS: "Watch Sarah bake your cookies! [LINK]"
5. Customer can see the actual baking process
6. After 30 min, link expires (customer can't rewind, can't screen record ‚Äî builds urgency)

**Why This Wins:**
- Builds trust (they see it's being made fresh)
- Creates viral marketing (customers share on Instagram/TikTok)
- Justifies premium pricing ("You saw me make it!")
- Increases perceived value (people pay more for artisanal feel)

**Technical Implementation:**
- Integration with Twitch or Mux.com
- Automatic quality detection (turns off stream if connection drops)
- Analytics: "Your 'Go Live' got 47 views, 12 shares"

#### Feature 3: Batch Delivery Routing

**Scenario:**
